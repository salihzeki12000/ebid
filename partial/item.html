Product Information
Stylish and smooth, the HTC Hero is a mid-range smartphone suitable for people of all ages. The HTC smartphone runs on Android 1.5 Cupcake OS that is packed with Google mobile services and also supports installation of preferred applications. The phone features a 3.2-inch HVGA resolution screen with LED backlight, delivering images with rich colors. The Android mobile has a 5 MP auto focus camera that lets you capture photographs in amazing quality. With 3G support and Wi-Fi connectivity on the HTC Hero, you can surf the web, check mails and stream videos. As the HTC smartphone comes with multiple messaging options such as text, picture, instant messaging and email, you can always stay in touch with your contacts. The Android mobile has an on-board GPS with A-GPS support to offer navigational assistance to reach your destination. With this Bluetooth-enabled HTC Hero, you can easily sync files with other portable devices.
