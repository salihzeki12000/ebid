Confidentiality
ebid will maintain the confidentiality of all information provided when a bidder submits its bid(s) to the extent provided by law.

  • The seller may not submit more than one bid per item in a product category.
  • Bids must follow the basic rule of:
    1. The product which has the price under $10 dollar, each bid must add $0.5 or more.
    2. The product which has the price between $10 and $50, each bid must add $1 or more.
    3. The product which has the price between $50 and $100, each bid must add $2 or more.
    4. The product which has the price between $100 and $300, each bid must add $5 or more.
    5. The product which has the price between $300 and $1000, each bid must add $10 or more.
    6. The product which has the price between $1000 and $10000, each bid must add $50 or more.
    7. The product which has the price between $10000 and $50000, each bid must add $500 or more.
    8. The product which has the price over $50000, each bid must add $1000 or more.
  • The seller can not participate the bidding, otherwise the administrator will lock the account.
  • ebid only offer a platform for trading, if the item wasn't as described, you can contact the seller or report to us.
  • The bid form requires sellers to identify the items for which they are bidding by manufacturer, model name, and number.
